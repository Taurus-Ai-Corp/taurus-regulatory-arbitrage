## TAURUS AI OEM AND WHITE-LABEL LICENSE AGREEMENT

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** December 4, 2024

This OEM and White-Label License Agreement ("Agreement") governs the terms under
which TAURUS AI Corp ("Licensor") grants white-label and OEM rights to authorized
partners ("Partner").

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "White-Label Rights"** means the right to rebrand, customize, and deploy
the Licensed Software under Partner's own brand identity.

**1.2 "OEM Rights"** means the right to embed the Licensed Software as a
component within Partner's own products or services.

**1.3 "End Users"** means Partner's customers who access the Licensed Software
through Partner's branded offering.

**1.4 "Revenue Share"** means the percentage of Partner's revenue from the
Licensed Software owed to Licensor as specified in the Order Form.

---

## 2. GRANT OF RIGHTS

**2.1 White-Label License Grant.** Licensor grants Partner:

(a) Non-exclusive right to rebrand the Licensed Software;
(b) Right to replace all TAURUS AI branding with Partner branding;
(c) Right to customize UI, colors, logos, and domain;
(d) Right to market the rebranded software as Partner's own product;
(e) Right to set pricing for End Users.

**2.2 OEM License Grant.** Licensor grants Partner:

(a) Right to embed Licensed Software in Partner's products;
(b) Right to distribute as component of Partner's offering;
(c) Right to create derivative works for integration;
(d) Right to provide support under Partner's brand.

**2.3 Source Code Access.** White-Label Partners receive:
- Full source code access
- Build and deployment scripts
- Documentation for customization
- API integration specifications

**8.1 Initial Term.** Minimum one (1) year term.

**8.2 Renewal.** Automatic renewal for successive one-year terms unless either
party provides 90 days written notice.

**8.3 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate upon:
- Material breach not cured within 30 days
- Bankruptcy or insolvency
- Change of control (requires consent)

**8.4 Wind-Down Period.** Upon termination:
- 180-day wind-down period to transition End Users
- Licensor will provide data export assistance
- Partner must remove all Licensor code within wind-down period
- Accrued revenue share remains payable

**8.5 Escrow Release.** For Enterprise+ tiers with code escrow:
- Escrow releases upon Licensor bankruptcy
- Escrow releases upon material breach by Licensor
